Stock Dashboard

Stock Dashboard is a personal local investment dashboard used by the account owner.

The app helps track portfolio holdings, trade confirmations, market data, and decision-support signals.

Gmail readonly access is used only to read emails labelled for depot import, so trade and depot confirmations can update the local portfolio.

Gmail send access is used only to send action summaries to the account owner.

Data is processed locally and is not sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.
