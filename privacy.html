Privacy Policy

Stock Dashboard is a personal, local investment dashboard used by the account owner.

The app uses Gmail access only to read emails labelled for depot import and to send action summaries to the account owner.

Data is processed locally and is not sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

The app may store local portfolio, trade confirmation, market data, and decision-support files on the user's own computer.
